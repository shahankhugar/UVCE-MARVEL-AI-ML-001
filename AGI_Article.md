# Artificial General Intelligence — The Next Frontier of AI

*A resource article on AGI, what it is, and why it matters.*

---

## What is AGI?

Today's AI systems are **narrow** — they do one thing well. ChatGPT writes text. AlphaFold folds proteins. But none of them can do everything a human can.

**Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)** is AI that can think, learn, and solve problems across any domain — just like a human.
```
ANI ──────────► AGI ──────────► ASI
Narrow AI      Human-level     Superhuman
(exists today)  (the goal)     (far future)
```

An AGI system could:
- **Accelerate science** — discover drugs, solve climate change
- **Automate complex work** — not just repetitive tasks
- **Reason and plan** — like a human expert in any field

---

## Key Challenges

| Challenge | What it means |
|-----------|--------------|
| **Catastrophic Forgetting** | AI forgets old skills when learning new ones |
| **Common Sense Reasoning** | AI doesn't understand the world like humans do |
| **Sample Efficiency** | AI needs millions of examples; humans need just a few |
| **Generalization** | AI struggles to apply knowledge to new situations |

## Is It Dangerous?

AGI raises serious concerns:

> *"AGI could be the last invention humanity ever needs to make — or the last one it gets to make."*

Risks include misuse, loss of control, and economic disruption. This is why **AI Safety** research is growing rapidly alongside capability research.

---

## When Will It Happen?

Nobody knows for sure:

- **Optimists** — 5 to 10 years (Sam Altman, Elon Musk)
- **Pessimists** — 50+ years or never
- **Most researchers** — somewhere in between

---

## Conclusion

AGI is not science fiction anymore — it is an active research goal at the world's leading AI labs. Understanding it now, while it is still being built, puts us in a better position to shape how it develops.
